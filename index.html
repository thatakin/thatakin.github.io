That Akın

Data Analyst and Researcher based in Suva, Fiji

Hey there! Welcome to my corner of the internet. I'm Akın, a Data Analyst and Researcher who loves diving into complex information and finding clear stories within it. I'm all about turning data into insights that actually help people make better decisions and that's what I used to do in UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency. I enjoy collaborating with others and finding creative ways to tackle challenges. Take a look around to see some of my work, let me know if you have any thoughts.

A Few Accomplishments

I worked across 5 continents on various crises, supporting data-driven decision-making and humanitarian response efforts. I am also an award-winning researcher (but I don't like to talk about that).

Research on Climate Displacement

What happens when the sea-levels rise and your country is underwater? Can you seek asylum if your home is destroyed because of a storm? How many people will be forced to migrate due to climate change? These are some of the questions I tried answering in the past and sometimes succeeded. Get in touch if you want to talk about climate mobility and displacement or climate migrants/refugees (whatever they mean).

Innovation and BeSci

How can you get more answers to your surveys? Can you "make" people change their habits? Is there a better way to collect a specific type of data? I led the development of innovative approaches to data and utilised BeSci to facilitate change in the past. I am always happy to talk about creative data processes that are supported by human-centered approaches.

Data in Crises

I managed all stages of data processing, from collection to analysis, visualisation to dissemination; as well as developing data strategies and data protection analyses in many crisis locations.

Some of My Work

Data is a powerful tool, but it can be overwhelming. I believe in making data accessible and understandable for everyone. My work focuses on creating clear, concise visualisations that tell a story and help people grasp complex information quickly.

Whether it's through interactive dashboards, infographics, or simple charts, I strive to present data in a way that is engaging and easy to digest. My goal is to empower people with the insights they need to make informed decisions.

Most of my work is available on UNHCR's Data Portal, like this one. After all it is important for everyone to get a sense of the reality with a quick glance.

I also code sometimes and have built apps like this: Bluesky Post Analyser. It is a simple tool for analysing your Bluesky posts. I built it on Python and posted on PythonAnywhere.

What can I Do for You?

If you have any research needs or if you need help with a data exercise, whether data collection, analysis or visualisation, I may be your guy. Get in touch in any way on the side and I will get back to you as soon as possible.
